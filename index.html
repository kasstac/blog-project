Neuralink A Neurotechnology Company

Author: Dev

Neuralink is a neurotechnology company developing implantable brain–machine interfaces (BMIs) founded by Elon Musk. It aims to enhance human cognition and memory, treat medical conditions like paralysis and depression, and enable human-AI interaction, potentially achieving symbiosis between humans and AI. Neuralink's technology involves implantable chips, thin flexible electrodes, and wireless communication, with the ultimate goal of merging human and artificial intelligence.
